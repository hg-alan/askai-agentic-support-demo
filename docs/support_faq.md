# Refund Policy
Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase if they provide a valid receipt.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

# Password Reset
To reset a password, direct the customer to the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
If they still can't access, escalate to Tier 2.

# Shipping Policy
Standard shipping takes 5-10 business days.
Express shipping takes 2-3 business days.
We do not ship hazardous materials internationally.

# Account Deletion
Customers can request account deletion via support.
We must respond within 30 days due to compliance obligations.
